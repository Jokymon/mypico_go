class components.Drive[source]¶

Bases: object

The drive object combines the two motors into one functionality for driving the robot.

backward(speed)[source]¶

Turn the motors on for backward motion. The speed is given in percent with 100% being the maximum backward speed equal to 100% PWM cycle.

forward(speed)[source]¶

Turn the motors on for forward motion. The speed is given in percent with 100% being the maximum forward speed equal to 100% PWM cycle.

left(speed)[source]¶

Turn the robot left with the given speed.

right(speed)[source]¶

Turn the robot right with the given speed.

set_motor(left, right)[source]¶

Set the motor speeds for the left and right motors individually simultaneously. The parameters left and right give the PWM percentages of the respective motors. Positive percentages will move the motors forward, while negative percentages will move the backwards.

stop()[source]¶

Stop all motors.
